About

Born in California, my professional and academic career has brought me around five different continents, but I'm now happy to call Seattle and the Pacific Northwest home. At MIT, I studied computer science and theater arts, and graduated with honors (Eta Kappa Nu and Tau Beta Pi). I've worked in research labs and startups building the next-generation human-computer experiences — including the MIT Media Lab, CSAIL, and ETH Zürich. I've also worked on cloud computing at Microsoft, blogged for MIT Admissions, and taught STEM workshops across Germany, Ghana, and Hong Kong.

As a professional figure skater, I trained and competed across the nation and world on Team USA, and picked up four national medals and two collegiate titles along the way. I'm now helping build the next-generation of skating talent with Sno-King Snoqualmie. Check out my features with US Figure Skating, MIT News, and Squarespace — and how about Kevin Shum Day?

Interested in the Seattle real estate market? I'm also a second-generation realtor with Coldwell Banker Bain. Ask me about special packages for first-time homebuyers. or book a free design or smart home consultation.
