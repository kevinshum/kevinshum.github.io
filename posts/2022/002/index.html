002 / Sunday scaries

2022

Tried Storyville Coffee at Pike Place for the first time and felt like a tourist. Not too crowded on a rainy Sunday morning. Small but cozy interior with views of the Sound. Very pricey cofee, a premium to be at the heart of the tourist attraction.

Also picked up a crumpet with smoked salmon cream cheese and egg. Did some driving through north Seattle and picked up some Scandinavian pastries at Larsen's Bakery and fish filet and sausages from Fresh Fish Co next door.

Spent the rest of the night writing from 6pm-1:30am. Once in the flow, just kept going.

Anxious about going back to work tomorrow after a nice holiday recharge. Already counting down until the next holiday.
